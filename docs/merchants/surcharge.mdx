# Surcharge disclosure

Stitch Payments can inform customers about credit card processing fees before they complete payment.

## Enable surcharge disclosure

Under **Other settings** in gateway configuration:

1. Enable **Enable Surcharge**.
2. Set **Surcharge Rate (%)** — for example `3.00` for 3%.
3. Save changes.

## What customers see

When a valid card is entered (or a saved card is selected), the checkout form can display:

- A calculating message while the fee is looked up
- A notice with the estimated processing fee, or
- A message that no fee applies to the card used

On classic checkout, a disclosure may also appear near the order totals. On Blocks checkout, a note can appear in the order summary sidebar.

## Important: disclosure vs. cart total

The surcharge feature is **disclosure-focused**. The displayed fee informs the customer; it is **not automatically added** as a WooCommerce cart fee in the order total shown at checkout. Confirm your legal and pricing requirements with [Stitch Payments](https://www.stitchpayments.net/) and your compliance team.

## Order records

When applicable, surcharge amounts can be stored on the order for reference in admin, emails, and order details.

## Testing

1. Enable surcharge and set a test rate.
2. Complete checkout with a test card in **Test Mode**.
3. Verify the notice appears and updates when switching between saved and new cards.
