# Stored payment methods

Let returning customers pay faster by saving cards to their account.

## Enable saved cards

In **WooCommerce → Settings → Payments → Stitch**, under **Other settings**:

1. Enable **Enable Stored Payment Methods**.
2. Save changes.

Customers will see a **Save card for future purchases** checkbox when paying with a new card.

## Customer experience

### At checkout

Logged-in customers with saved cards see a list of cards on the payment form. They can:

- Select a saved card, or
- Choose **Use a new card** to enter fresh details

### My Account

Customers manage saved methods under **My Account → Payment methods**:

- View saved cards
- Add a new payment method
- Remove cards they no longer need

## Subscriptions

WooCommerce Subscriptions renewal orders require a reusable payment method. When subscriptions are enabled, saved tokens are used for scheduled renewals.

## Data and privacy

Saved cards use WooCommerce payment tokens with CardPointe profile and account identifiers — not full card numbers. CVV is never stored.

See [Data and privacy](/docs/merchants/data-and-privacy) for tokenization, order metadata, logging, uninstall options, and privacy-policy guidance.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Check |
|-------|-------|
| Save checkbox missing | **Enable Stored Payment Methods** is on and customer is logged in |
| Saved cards not listed | Customer has tokens for gateway `stitch`; profile/acct IDs must be present |
| Guest checkout | Gateway currently requires login at checkout for new payments |
